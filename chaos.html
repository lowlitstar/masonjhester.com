to write about chaos
is to write the absolute

she rests center stage, she plays the part
in every corner of the cosmos and beyond
in the midnight moon, in blossoming flowers
and in the violent afternoon thunderstorms

she will forge herself interwoven among stars
within unseen shadows of towering concrete,
exhausting cascades of collided planets
lowlit alleys of supernovae,
enigmatic incantations of defiance

so, to dream of control is hopeless
her crown rests endlessly upon her head

intensely terrifying;
overwhelmingly beautiful.
