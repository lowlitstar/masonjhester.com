there is no one here
to witness these bright strides of creation,
and though an audience may rightfully feel imperative here,
the transient surfaces which you see are merely
hollow platforms of raging turbulence; not yet imbued
with the fundamental systems that soon, will
flood this world with life.

this instinctive longing to feel at place here is an
unsurprising, but fleeting working of a history of
repeated, boundless worlds.

here, just as the rest,
you will most importantly be treated with
supreme and utmost reverence; because,
like lush flowers gone unwatered,
to forget & neglect is to
regrettably condemn her to falter.

and so to act unjustly here, would undermine this
eternally endless, disastrously divine, blazing,
thunderous reign.
